Integrations

⚡ Make / Zapier

Connect Make.com or Zapier scenarios via webhooks. Paste your scenario trigger URLs in the Ad Manager and Lead Engine panels.

📧 Email

Route lead notifications & follow-ups through your email provider. Use a tool like Make to relay from this webhook.

💬 SMS (Twilio / OpenPhone)

Fire SMS sequences via Make + Twilio or OpenPhone. Configure number and auth in your Make scenario.

📊 Google Analytics

Paste your GA4 Measurement ID to track form fills and conversions from your M&T website.

⭐ Review Requests

Auto-send Google review requests after confirmed jobs. Connect via Make to your Google Business Profile.

🤖 AI Receptionist

Link your M&T AI receptionist to push conversations into this dashboard as leads automatically.
