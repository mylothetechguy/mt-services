1. The agreement

This agreement sets the shared understanding between M&T Services ("we", "us") and [Business Name] ("you", "the Client"). It replaces any prior verbal understanding. Any changes must be agreed to in writing by both parties.

2. Scope of work

We will deliver the service(s) you have purchased:

Website Build — $1,499
  • Custom design & build
  • SEO optimization
  • Professional copywriting
  • Contact forms & lead capture
  • Hosting & domain setup
  • Ongoing maintenance & support
  • 2 rounds of revisions included
AI Virtual Receptionist — $499 + $250/mo
  • Custom-trained AI assistant
  • Answers, books & captures leads 24/7
  • After-hours & weekend coverage
  • Review monitoring & management
  • Monthly performance report
  • Ongoing retraining included

Work not listed above is considered an add-on and will be quoted separately before any work begins.

3. Timeline & client responsibilities

  • Work begins once payment clears and your content/assets are received.
  • You agree to provide materials, feedback, and approvals promptly. Delays in materials shift the timeline accordingly.
  • You confirm you own (or are licensed to use) all logos, images, and copy you provide.

4. Payments

  • Website Build: $1,499 due in full before work begins.
  • AI Receptionist: $499 setup fee due before onboarding begins (non-refundable once onboarding starts), then $250/mo recurring.
  • Monthly retainer is billed in advance and is cancel-anytime with 30 days' written notice.
  • Invoices unpaid after 14 days may result in a pause in services.

5. Ownership & revisions

  • Website Build: on final payment, you own the delivered website files outright.
  • AI Receptionist: the AI configuration remains active as part of your monthly plan. On cancellation, your conversation history can be exported within 30 days.
  • Revisions: your website build includes 2 rounds of revisions. Additional rounds are billed at our current hourly rate, quoted in advance.

6. Confidentiality & portfolio

We keep your business information confidential. Unless you opt out in writing, we may showcase the finished website in our portfolio and marketing materials.
