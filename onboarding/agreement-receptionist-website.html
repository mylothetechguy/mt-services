1. The agreement

This agreement sets the shared understanding between M&T Services ("we", "us") and [Business Name] ("you", "the Client") for the AI Virtual Receptionist + Free Website package. It replaces any prior verbal understanding. Any changes must be agreed to in writing by both parties.

2. What's included

AI Virtual Receptionist — $499 + $250/mo
  • Custom-trained AI assistant in your brand voice
  • Answers, books & captures leads 24/7
  • After-hours & weekend coverage
  • Lead routing & ongoing retraining
  • Monthly performance report
Professional Website + Hosting — FREE
  • Custom, mobile-friendly website
  • Domain connection & secure hosting
  • Contact forms & lead capture
  • Ongoing upkeep & minor edits
  • Included free while your plan is active (a $1,499 value)

Work not listed above is considered an add-on and will be quoted separately before any work begins.

3. Investment & payments

  • One-time setup: $499 due before onboarding begins (non-refundable once onboarding starts).
  • Monthly plan: $250/mo, billed in advance starting at signing and renewing on the same date each month. This covers your AI Receptionist and your website hosting & maintenance.
  • Website + hosting are included at no extra cost for as long as your monthly plan stays active.
  • The monthly plan is cancel-anytime with 30 days' written notice.
  • Invoices unpaid after 14 days may result in a pause in services.

4. Timeline & your responsibilities

  • Work begins once the $499 setup payment clears and your content/assets are received.
  • You agree to provide materials, feedback, and approvals promptly. Delays in materials shift the timeline accordingly.
  • You confirm you own (or are licensed to use) all logos, images, and copy you provide.

5. The "free" website — how it works

  • Because the website is provided free as part of your plan, M&T Services hosts and maintains it for you while you are an active monthly subscriber.
  • If your monthly plan ends, the included website & AI hosting also end. We will keep your site live for 14 days after cancellation so you can transition.
  • On request within that window, we will export your content and, where possible, help you migrate to your own hosting (migration assistance may be quoted as an add-on).
  • Prefer to own your website outright instead? Ask us about our one-time Website Build — your content and files become yours on final payment.

6. Ownership, confidentiality & portfolio

  • Your business content, leads, and conversation history belong to you and can be exported within 30 days of cancellation.
  • We keep your business information confidential.
  • Unless you opt out in writing, we may showcase the finished website in our portfolio and marketing.
