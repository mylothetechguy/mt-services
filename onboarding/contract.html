2. Payment terms

2.1 — Setup fee

Any one-time setup fee listed above is due in full before work commences. For the AI Virtual Receptionist, this is $499.99 and is non-refundable once onboarding has begun.

2.2 — Monthly retainer

Monthly retainer fees are billed in advance on the same calendar date each month. For the AI Virtual Receptionist, the recurring retainer is $250.00 per month. Failure to pay within 7 days of the due date may result in suspension of services.

2.3 — Cancellation

Monthly services are cancel-anytime with 30 days' written notice sent to mylesjwells@mtservicesusa.com. The Client remains responsible for any retainer fees that fall within the 30-day notice period. No partial-month refunds are issued.

2.4 — Late payments

Invoices unpaid after 14 days may incur a late fee of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance. M&T Services reserves the right to pause services until the account is current.

3. Scope & deliverables

3.1 — Included work

M&T Services will deliver the services checked in Section 1. All work is as described in any accompanying proposal or service description. Requests outside this scope will be quoted separately before work begins.

3.2 — AI Virtual Receptionist specifics

The AI Virtual Receptionist will be trained on information provided by the Client. The Client agrees to provide accurate business details, FAQs, and service information during onboarding. M&T Services is not liable for responses generated from inaccurate Client-supplied data. Retraining requests due to changes in the Client's business are included in the monthly retainer.

3.3 — Revisions

Project-based deliverables include 2 rounds of revisions. Additional revisions are billed at the then-current hourly rate, quoted in advance.

3.4 — Timeline

M&T Services will work diligently to meet any agreed timelines. Delays caused by the Client (late content, approvals, or access) will shift the project timeline accordingly. M&T Services is not liable for delays outside its reasonable control.

4.2 — Subscription services
